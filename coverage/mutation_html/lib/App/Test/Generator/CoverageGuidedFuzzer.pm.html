   64: =head1 DESCRIPTION
   65: 
   66: Implements coverage-guided fuzzing on top of App::Test::Generator's
   67: existing schema-driven input generation. Instead of purely random
   68: generation it:
   69: 
   70: =over 4
   71:

   75:

   77:

   79: 
   80: =back

   82: This is the Perl equivalent of what AFL/libFuzzer do at the byte level,
   83: but operating on typed, schema-validated Perl data structures.
